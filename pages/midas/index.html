  • How to start investing?

    • You can start investing with Midas.Investments in just three steps.

Please pay attention to all notes when depositing to ensure you're depositing using the correct blockchain. Your first reward will pay out exactly 24 hours after your first deposit (and will then autocompound each day). Our "Swap" feature allows you to swap between supported assets in just a few simple clicks.

  • Is the Midas.Investments secure?

    There are two easy ways to log in to Midas.Investments: Discord and Gmail. Each of these services, which include an IP-validation check and many other security layers, provide for excellent security when paired with 2FA. Additionally, there's a spot in your Profile to provide a verification email, which is where requests would go for all withdrawals from your account.Our database sensitive data is encrypted with AES-256 security, and the decryption key has a regular rotation, which ensures the security of database even if a threat were to receive access to it.

  • How are funds withdrawn from the platform?

    In order to withdraw funds, follow these steps:

    • First, you must select the wallet address where the funds will be transferred: this can be the address of an external exchange, local wallet or other service.
    • Open your investment unit card and find there the field for the withdrawal address, and next to it the "EDIT" button.
    • Once you have provided a withdrawal address, you need to confirm the withdrawal address by email. The address will change only after your confirmation.

  • What is Midas token and what are its values?

    MIDAS is the utility token of Midas.Investments that serves as a bridge between CeFi & DeFi and governance token for Midas DeFi protocol. It is an ERC20 token based on the Ethereum blockchain and is capped at a 5 million total supply. MIDAS is used in nearly every facet of Midas.Investments, including the Payout Split program, Midas Boost, burn from CeDeFi fees, and the governance process for managing Midas DeFi protocol.
